How It Works

1
Register & Verify

Create your account as a Farmer or a Wholesaler. Provide your basic details and let our admin team instantly verify your credentials for safety.

Farmers can use our smart terminal to list crop harvests or idle tractors. Wholesalers browse the vast nationwide map to find the best yields.
